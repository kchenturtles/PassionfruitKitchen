Ube Mooncakes
Mooncakes are a symbolic dessert eaten on the Mid Autumn Festival. They are decorated with elaborate stamped designs and characters on top. Traditionally, mooncakes are baked pastries with a rich filling. The pastry layer is akin to a soft cookie, while the filling can be savory or sweet. Traditional fillings include lotus seed paste with salted egg yolk, red bean paste, mixed nutes, and jujube or date paste.
